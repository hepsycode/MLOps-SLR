# Snowballing Candidate Full Texts

This directory contains local copies of the studies identified during citation chasing before the final snowballing full-text decision.

The files are separated according to how they were discovered:

- [`Backward/`](Backward/): studies found in the reference lists of seed or selected papers.
- [`Forward/`](Forward/): studies found by identifying papers that cite seed or selected papers.

These folders contain candidates, not the final snowballing evidence base. The screening decisions are recorded in the parent directory, and the 17 studies retained for full-text assessment are copied to [`../selected-snowballing/`](../selected-snowballing/).

## Naming convention

Each PDF begins with a stable local identifier (`SB01` to `SB33`) followed by a shortened bibliographic title. The same identifier is used in CSV/XLSX screening records and, when the study is included, in the data extraction dataset and manuscript citation key.
